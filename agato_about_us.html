What is Agato?

We help organizations identify and reduce risk before it becomes an incident.

Agato is building an advanced, scalable cybersecurity platform designed for small and mid-sized businesses that need enterprise-grade security capabilities without enterprise-sized security teams. Agato focuses on attack surface reduction, infrastructure hardening, continuous monitoring, and proactive defense measures that help organizations identify and remediate security risks before they become active incidents. The platform is especially designed for IT departments that may only have one to three team members and are often responsible for managing complex infrastructure, security operations, compliance expectations, and business continuity with limited time and resources.

Agato helps close that gap by applying modern capabilities from software engineering, cybersecurity, automation, and AI/ML. Its services and platform capabilities include external attack surface analysis, shadow IT discovery, open-source intelligence aggregation, infrastructure exposure monitoring, malware and threat campaign awareness, penetration testing, adversarial emulation, and network traffic analysis. By combining traditional security techniques with machine learning-driven detection and analysis, Agato gives smaller organizations the ability to see their environment more clearly, understand where they are exposed, and prioritize the risks that matter most.

Operating Model

Centralized intelligence, technical validation, and timely alerting.

At its core, Agato is intended to be a force multiplier for lean IT and security teams. Instead of requiring clients to manually collect data from fragmented tools, interpret threat intelligence, pay for numerous subscriptions to access data or continuously monitor exposed infrastructure on their own, Agato provides a centralized capability for situational awareness, technical validation, and timely alerting. Through real-time IT and leadership notifications, continuous infrastructure monitoring, automated testing, OSINT enrichment, and tailored security analysis, Agato aims to serve as a one-stop security partner for organizations trying to stay ahead of sophisticated cyber actors.
